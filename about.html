About

A small studio, real work in the open.

Safi Studio is a small dev-tools studio led by Safi, a senior and lead software engineer. We build libraries, extensions, and internal tools across Laravel, .NET, Node.js, VS Code, and Obsidian. It's where our tools live: the open-source work we ship for the community, and the tooling builds we take on for agencies that need senior hands without a long hire.

The proof isn't a testimonial wall. It's LaralCN-UI running live, plugins you can install, and packages you can read. You judge the work before you ever email us.

Because we're small, you stay close to the people building the thing from the first call to the handover. No account managers, no telephone game between you and the code.
