Who it's for

Agencies and product teams on Laravel that ship multiple client sites a year and rebuild the same UI each time. If your devs copy-paste components from the last project and tweak them by hand, this pays for itself fast.

Not for you if you're on a single long-lived app with a mature design system already in place. In that case the internal-tool build is probably the better fit.
